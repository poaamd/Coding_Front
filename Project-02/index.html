La Liga

La Liga, officially known as LaLiga Santander, is Spain's top professional football division, featuring 20 teams competing annually for the championship. Established in 1929, it's one of the most prestigious football leagues globally, renowned for its high level of competition and world-class players. Legendary clubs like FC Barcelona and Real Madrid dominate the league, attracting millions of fans worldwide. Teams play a double round-robin format, with the top teams qualifying for European competitions like the UEFA Champions League, while the lowest-ranked teams face relegation to Segunda División. La Liga’s exciting matches, fierce rivalries, and footballing talent make it a centerpiece of global football culture.


Records

La Liga has seen some remarkable records over the years, highlighting the league's rich history and talent. Here are a few notable records:

  • Most Titles: Real Madrid holds the record for the most La Liga titles, having won it 35 times, followed by FC Barcelona with 27 titles.
  • All-Time Top Scorer: Lionel Messi is the league's all-time top scorer with 474 goals, all scored while playing for FC Barcelona.
  • Most Goals in a Single Season: Messi also holds the record for most goals in a single La Liga season, scoring 50 goals in the 2011–2012 season.
  • Most Appearances: Andoni Zubizarreta, a former goalkeeper, has made the most appearances in La Liga with 622 games played.
  • Biggest Win: Athletic Bilbao's 12-1 victory against Barcelona in 1931 remains the biggest win in La Liga history.
  • Most Consecutive Titles: Real Madrid and Barcelona share the record for most consecutive titles, both having won the league five times in a row.

Sponsors

  • Spotify – Main shirt sponsor and naming rights partner for the Camp Nou stadium since 2022.
  • Nike – Official kit manufacturer, a partnership that began in 1998.
  • Beko – Sleeve sponsor and partner in various categories, such as home appliances.
  • WhiteBIT – Cryptocurrency exchange platform, became an official sponsor in 2023.
  • Rakuten – Previously the main shirt sponsor (2017-2022); now a global partner.
  • Konami – Video game developer, involved with the club’s digital and gaming initiatives.
  • Scotiabank – Official bank sponsor in Latin America.
